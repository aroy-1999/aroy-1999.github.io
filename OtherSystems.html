Other Systems

 

 

 

When evaluating other goose deterrent systems, is important to keep a few things in mind:

  • Geese visit at all times of the day; there is no sacred hour.
  • Geese are creatures of habit; they keep returning to the same area.
  • Geese are persistent; they keep testing deterrent systems until they find a weakness.
  • Humans may need to regularly use the space where a deterrent system is set up.
  • Humans can not be relied on to perform regular tasks; they forget.

The Smart Goose Deterrent System is completely automated, runs 24/7, is people friendly, and keeps geese away.
