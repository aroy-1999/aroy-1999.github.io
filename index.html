Smart Goose Deterrent System

 

 

Solution

The Smart Goose Deterrent System uses cameras, Object Recognition AI, and sprinklers to keep geese away. Other deterrents just don't work. Geese are too smart. However, they can be outsmarted with AI.

 

SGDS in Action

When SGDS detects geese, it activates sprinklers. People and dogs are safe to enjoy the protected and clean space. (see how SGDS works)
