Solution

The Smart Goose Deterrent System uses cameras, AI, and sprinklers to keep geese away. Geese are too smart for other deterrents, so we decided to outsmart them.

When the SGDS AI detects geese, it activates sprinklers. People and dogs are safe to enjoy the protected and poop-free space. (see how SGDS works)
