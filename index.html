Smart Goose Deterrent System

Problem

Tired of geese invading your dock or lawn and leaving droppings?

Solution

The Smart Goose Deterrent System (SGDS) uses cameras and AI to detect geese, then automatically activates sprinklers to send them away. SGDS outsmarts geese before they make a mess. (see how it works)

Why SGDS is Effective

Unlike other deterrents, SGDS:

  • reacts specifically to geese;
  • avoids people and pets; and
  • works automatically, 24/7 requiring minimal human intervention.
