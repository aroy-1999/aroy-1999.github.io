How SGDS Works

Solution

The Smart Goose Deterrent System captures a photo every few seconds and sends it to the cloud. Photos are processed using motion detection and Object Recognition AI. If SGDS finds geese and no people, it activates sprinklers.

Object Recognition AI

Object Recognition AI is pretty good at finding geese and people, but not perfect. SGDS adds additional rules, so there is little risk of missing a goose or spraying a person.

No Humans Required

We believe humans are the weakest link in other goose deterrent systems. Humans forget to perform regular maintenance. That's why SGDS is completely automatic and runs 24/7.

Sprinklers

Sprinklers are an effective and humane deterrent. Geese do NOT like being sprayed!
