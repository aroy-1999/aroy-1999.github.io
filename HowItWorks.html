How SGDS Works

 

 

Solution

The Smart Goose Deterrent System captures a photo every few seconds and sends it to the cloud. Photos are processed using motion detection, image recognition, and AI. If SGDS finds geese, it activates sprinklers. If it finds people or nothing of interest, it disables sprinklers.

 

Image Recognition and AI

Image recognition and AI are pretty good at finding geese and people. SGDS adds pre and postprocessing rules; so in the end, there is little risk of missing a goose or spraying a person.

No Humans Required

We believe humans are the weakest link in other goose deterrent systems. They forget to perform regular tasks. That's why SGDS requires no human support. It is completely automatic and runs 24/7.

 

 

Sprinklers

Sprinklers are an effective and humane deterrent. Geese do NOT like being sprayed!
