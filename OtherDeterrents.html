Smart Goose Deterrent System vs. Other Goose Deterrents

When evaluating other goose deterrent systems, is important to keep a few things in mind:

  • Geese are smart; they figure out when deterrents are not a threat.
  • Humans forget to perform regular tasks.
  • Geese and humans may share the same space; be careful NOT to deter humans by accident.

The SGDS is completely automated, runs 24/7, is people and pet friendly, and keeps geese away.
